# Welcome to Pause Urbaine

Your hair salon in Geneva, with two locations to serve you better.

## Our Salons

We are pleased to welcome you to our two salons located in Plainpalais and Eaux-Vives. Each salon offers a warm atmosphere and professional hair services tailored to your needs.

## Our Philosophy

At Pause Urbaine, we believe that every salon visit should be a well-deserved break in your day. Our team of experienced hairdressers is dedicated to providing you not only with a perfect cut, but also with a moment of relaxation and well-being.

## Professional Services

We offer a complete range of hair services for men and women, including cuts, coloring, highlights, hair treatments, and special occasion styling.
